Archery Lessons

Kenosha Bowmen is pleased to offer archery classes for individuals and families. This is a beginning level archery class taught by certified National Archery Association Level 1 instructors. Classes consist of 3 sessions that will last 1 1/2 hours. We utilize Genesis (click here to view) compound bows for our classes. If you already have a bow, please feel free to bring it to class. The cost for the class is $ per family.

Who

The program is open to the public. You do not need to be a member to participate. Children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult who also participates in the class. The focus of the class is family archery. The entire families are encouraged to participate together. The only limitations for our youngest participants are that they be mature enough to focus for 1 1/2 of class activity and strong enough to independently pull and hold our mini bows. Recommended age minimum 8 years old.

When

  • Family Archery classes meet 1 day a week for 3 weeks.
  • This is a 3 class session that meets from 7pm - 8:30pm.
  • Class size is limited.
