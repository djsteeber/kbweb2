Archery Lessons

What

Kenosha Bowmen is pleased to offer archery classes for individuals and families.

  • This is a beginning level archery class taught by certified National Archery Association Level 1 instructors
  • Class sessions will last an hour-and-a-half
  • There will be three sessions in the series
  • We utilize Genesis (click here to view) compound bows for our classes
  • If you already have a bow, please feel free to bring it to class
  • The cost for the class is $100 per family

Who

  • The program is open to the public
  • Children under 16 must be accompanied by an adult who also participates in the class
  • The focus of the class is family archery
  • The entire families are encouraged to participate together
  • The only limitation for our youngest participants is that they be mature enough to focus on the hour-and-a-half of class activity and strong enough to independently pull and hold our mini bows
  • Recommended age minimum 8 years old

When

  • Family Archery classes meet 1 day a week for 3 weeks.
  • Our next class is scheduled for Tuesday nights on March 10th, 17th and 24th.
  • This is a 3 class session that meets from 7pm - 8:30pm.
  • Class size is limited.

How

To reserve a spot please
  • Email lessons@kenoshabowmen.com
  • In the email please list the names and ages of the archers that will be taking the class
  • The cost is $100 for the entire family
  • A response from one of our club members will be emailed to confirm your class reservation, along with additional information
