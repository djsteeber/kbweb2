Membership

The Kenosha Bowmen have an open membership policy.

To become a member you must fulfill the following requirements:

  • Fill out this Membership Application Form.
  • Pay a one time $125 Initiation Fee.
  • Pay $80 annual dues.
  • Pay $25 Wisconsin Bowhunters annual membership fee.
  • Have your application signed by a member/sponsor.
  • Attend a regular meeting to be voted into membership. (First Sunday of every month.)

As a member you have the following privileges:

  • Access to the front gate and clubhouse.
  • Use of all our ranges and facilities for you and your immediate family.
  • Members rates at local archery shoots.

As a member you have the following obligations:

  • Pay annual dues on time.
  • Work at least 20 hours for the benefit of the club per year. (Prorated for mid-year applicants)
